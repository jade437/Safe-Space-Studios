Creating a Safety Plan

Whether you're experiencing harm or working to change harmful behaviors, safety planning is essential.

1

Identify Safe Spaces

Determine places where you can go if you feel unsafe, including friends' homes or public places.

2

Prepare Emergency Contacts

Keep important phone numbers handy, including trusted friends, family, and support services.

3

Plan for Escalation

Identify triggers and warning signs, and create strategies to de-escalate situations.

4

Secure Important Documents

Keep copies of IDs, financial records, and other important documents in a safe place.

5

Develop Self-Care Strategies

Identify activities and practices that help you manage stress and maintain emotional wellbeing.
