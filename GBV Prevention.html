Understanding Different Forms of Gender-Based Violence

GBV includes various forms of harm and control. Recognizing these patterns is the first step toward safety.

Physical Abuse

Hitting, slapping, pushing, choking, or any physical force used to control or harm.

Emotional Abuse

Verbal attacks, humiliation, intimidation, isolation, and controlling behaviors.

Financial Abuse

Controlling access to money, preventing employment, or exploiting financial resources.

Digital Abuse

Monitoring communications, demanding passwords, or using technology to control or harass.
